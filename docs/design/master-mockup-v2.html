Bring your spouse home to the UK — without the refusal roulette

Every year thousands of applications fail on paperwork, not merit. This guide walks the route we use in practice — and where a professional pair of eyes earns its fee.

The financial requirement

The minimum income is £29,000 ✦ STALE — REFRESH DRAFTED — met through salary, savings, or a combination. The evidence rules are unforgiving: six months of payslips, matching bank statements, employer letter.

The relationship evidence

Genuine-and-subsisting is a legal test, not a vibe. Photographs help; joint tenancy, shared finances and travel history decide.
